Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How fresh are your fruits?

All our fruits are harvested within 48 hours of delivery. We work directly with farms to ensure maximum freshness.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all major cities in the US. Enter your zip code at checkout to confirm availability.

Can I customize my subscription box?

Absolutely! You can set preferences, exclude allergies, and choose your delivery frequency in your account settings.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with any fruit, we'll replace it or refund you - no questions asked.
